Disease info is a web app for
Nigerians to get information on
diseases which would help prevent them

Made with by Devcenter-Square

Why

The top diseases that kill Nigerians are diseases that can be easily prevented and also cured.

And if people knew the right information on these diseases, causes and what they can do to prevent them, it would go a long way.

Disease Info was built to make that possible.
