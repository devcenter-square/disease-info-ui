Why

The top diseases that kill Nigerians are diseases that can be easily prevented and also cured.

If people knew the right information on these diseases, causes of infections, and what they can be done ro prevent them, it would go a long way.

Disease Info was built so as to make information on major diseases affecting Nigerians easily accessible.

Disease info is a web app for
Nigerians to get information on
diseases and how to prevent them
